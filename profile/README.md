## What Is AlDente for Mac

AlDente mac is a menu bar tool that lets you limit your MacBook from charging above a certain percentage.
Keeping a lithium battery pinned at 100 percent accelerates wear, so this battery charge limiter mac holds the
charge lower to decrease wear and increase the longevity of your battery.

If you've never used a tool like this, don't worry — it does one simple thing, and this beginner-friendly
tutorial walks you through it from the very first step. By the end you'll understand how AlDente for mac
protects long-term battery health mac without changing how you use your Mac.

## AlDente for Beginners

Here's the idea in plain terms. Batteries wear fastest when they're kept full and warm. Most of us leave a
MacBook plugged in all day, so the battery sits at 100 percent for hours — exactly the condition that ages it.

AlDente mac fixes that by putting a ceiling on charging. You pick a percentage, and the battery charge limiter
mac stops there instead of topping all the way up. That's the whole concept: less time at full charge means a
healthier battery over the years. You don't need to understand the chemistry to benefit from it — you just set
a limit once.
